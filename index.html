Himalayan Hope Foundation Nepal (HHFN) works at the grassroots to bring education, health, and sustainable opportunities to remote communities across Nepal.

About Us

Mission

Empower rural communities through quality education, skill development, and sustainable livelihood programs in Nepal.

Vision

Equal opportunities for all in Nepal — where every child learns, every woman thrives, and nature is protected.

Our Story

Founded after the 2015 earthquake, HHFN started with a small school rebuild and now serves 20+ villages with holistic development.

Programs & Projects

Education Support

Scholarships, school supplies, and digital classrooms for remote Himalayan children.

Women Empowerment

Tailoring, entrepreneurship, and leadership workshops for rural women.

Sustainable Tourism

Community homestays & eco-trekking to preserve culture and generate income.

Health & Awareness

Mobile health camps, sanitation, and hygiene education.

Environment Protection

Tree plantation, waste management, and climate resilience initiatives.

Meet the Founders & Leadership

Gaurab Sapkota

Founder & Executive Director

Former social worker and mountaineer, Gaurab founded HHFN after witnessing the educational gaps in rural Nepal. He leads with 2+ years of grassroots development experience.

Sita Sharma

Co-Founder & Program Director

Sita is a women's rights advocate and education specialist. She drives all women empowerment and child education initiatives across remote districts.

Karma Sherpa

Head of Operations

Karma manages field logistics, sustainable tourism projects, and community relations, ensuring every program reaches the most vulnerable families.

Dr. Anjali Thapa

Health & Awareness Director

Dr. Anjali leads mobile health camps and sanitation drives, bringing essential healthcare to remote Himalayan villages.
