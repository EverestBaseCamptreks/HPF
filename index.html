About Us

Who We Are

HealthFirst Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to improving global health outcomes through innovative programs, community engagement, and partnerships with healthcare providers.

Founded in 2005, we have impacted over 2 million lives across 30 countries by providing access to essential healthcare services, health education, and disease prevention programs.

Our team of healthcare professionals, volunteers, and partners work tirelessly to bridge the gap in healthcare accessibility and quality for vulnerable populations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To improve health equity by providing accessible, affordable, and quality healthcare services to underserved communities while promoting health education and preventive care.

Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of socioeconomic status or geographic location, has access to the healthcare they need to live a healthy and productive life.

Our Values

Compassion, Integrity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Accountability guide everything we do at HealthFirst Foundation.

Our Activities

Mobile Medical Camps

We organize mobile medical camps in remote areas to provide free consultations, basic treatments, and health screenings to communities with limited access to healthcare facilities.

Health Education Programs

Our community health workers conduct workshops on nutrition, hygiene, disease prevention, and maternal health to empower individuals with knowledge for better health outcomes.

Vaccination Drives

We partner with local health authorities to organize vaccination campaigns against preventable diseases, especially targeting children in low-income communities.

Clean Water Initiatives

Our clean water projects install filtration systems and wells in communities lacking access to safe drinking water, significantly reducing waterborne diseases.

Maternal Health Services

We provide prenatal and postnatal care, safe delivery assistance, and maternal health education to reduce maternal and infant mortality rates in underserved areas.

Disaster Relief

Our emergency response teams provide medical aid, supplies, and support to communities affected by natural disasters or health crises.
